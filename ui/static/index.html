    System overview

    Longitudinal EHR visits are encoded with a Transformer, drug interactions with a DDI-aware GAT, fused via a learnable gate, then scored for safe multi-label recommendations.

    1

    Clinical embedding

    Diagnoses, medications, and labs per visit → unified visit vectors.

    2

    Temporal encoder

    Transformer with padding mask; pools the last valid visit for patient state.

    3

    DDI-aware GAT

    Graph attention over drug nodes; patient drug context from latest meds.

    4

    Gated fusion

    Balances temporal patient signal vs. drug-graph signal.

    5

    Recommendation head

    Multi-label sigmoid outputs with BCE + DDI-aware penalty.

    6

    DeepSHAP

    Feature attribution for diagnoses, medications, and labs.
